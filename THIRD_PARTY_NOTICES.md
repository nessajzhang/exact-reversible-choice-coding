# Third-party data and licence boundary

The BSD-3-Clause licence in `LICENSE` applies to the original software in the Paper 2 release. It does not relicense:

- public PCR measurements, released model predictions, notebooks or sequence-property files from the upstream `BorgwardtLab/PCR-bias` repository;
- public DT4DDS inputs or other third-party datasets;
- third-party software, model weights or documentation; or
- manuscript text and figures unless a separate rights statement explicitly says otherwise.

Upstream inputs remain at their cited public sources and are verified by repository URL, commit or version, file path and SHA-256 digest. Users must follow the upstream terms for those materials. The public release distributes author-derived code, tests, manifests and source-data products needed to audit the reported analyses; it does not claim ownership of reused measurements.
